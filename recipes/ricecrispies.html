Rice Crispies

Rice Crispies Ingredients

These are the kitchen staples you'll need to make the best Rice Crispies of your life:

  • 1/4 cup of butter

  • 4 cups of minature marshmallows

  • 5 cups of Rice Crispy cereal

Rice Crispies Baking Instructions

Here are the steps you will need to ensure the best quality squares

  1. Melt butter in a large saucepan over low heat.

  2. Add marshmallows and stir until melted and well combined. Cook 2 minutes longer, stirring constantly. Remove from heat.

  3. Stir in crispy rice cereal until well coated.

  4. Press mixture evenly and firmly into a buttered 9x13-inch pan using a buttered spatula or waxed paper.

  5. Cut into 2-inch squares when cool.
